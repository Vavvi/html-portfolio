My Favourite Movies Of All Time

Top 3 Rankings

Shutter Island

Personally, I think shutter island is one of Leonardo DiCaprios best films. His performance is unmatched by any other, and the twist ending has stayed with me ever since I watched it.

Spirited Away

The first studio ghibli movie I ever watched, and I fell in love with it as a child. The soundtrack and animation style is breathtaking, and really feels magical.

Coraline

Coraline is one of the reasons I fell in love with stop motion movies. The creepy atmosphere created is seriously unmatched by anthing else.
